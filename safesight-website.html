Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Care

From routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures, our team delivers precision outcomes with compassionate care.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification with premium intraocular lens implantation. Restore crystal-clear vision with South Africa's most experienced surgical team.

LASIK & PRK

Wavefront-guided laser vision correction tailored to your unique eye profile. Reduce or eliminate your dependence on glasses and contact lenses.

Glaucoma Treatment

Early detection and advanced minimally invasive glaucoma surgery (MIGS). Protect your vision from the silent thief of sight with expert intervention.

Retina Care

Comprehensive retinal diagnosis and treatment including vitrectomy, laser therapy, and macular degeneration management using the latest imaging technology.

Paediatric Ophthalmology

Specialised eye care for children including strabismus correction, amblyopia treatment, and congenital eye conditions managed with gentle expertise.

General Eye Health

Comprehensive eye examinations, diabetic eye screening, dry eye management, and routine ophthalmic care for lasting visual health at every stage of life.
