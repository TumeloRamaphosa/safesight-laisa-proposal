# Digital Partnership Proposal

**Studex Group → SafeSight Aesthetic Clinic**

---

## Executive Summary

Studex Group proposes a full-service digital partnership for SafeSight Aesthetic Clinic. We will design, build, and manage your complete digital infrastructure — from patient-facing website and social media to internal AI operations and automated booking — all controlled through a single unified dashboard, the LAISA Agent OS.

This is not a one-time project. It is a recurring-revenue SaaS partnership engineered to drive patient acquisition, build brand authority in the South African aesthetic medicine market, and eliminate operational overhead through AI automation.

---

## Scope of Work

### Phase A — Initial Build (One-Time)

Delivered within 2–3 weeks of kickoff. This phase constructs every digital asset SafeSight requires to operate and compete online.

| Deliverable | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **LAISA Agent OS Dashboard** | Unified internal operations dashboard — patient CRM, appointment calendar, analytics, agent controls, and content pipeline in one interface. |
| **Clinic Website (Vercel)** | Google-optimized, mobile-first clinic website with service pages, booking integration, and patient trust architecture. |
| **Google Ads Account + Campaign** | Full account setup, keyword research, and launch of first search campaign targeting high-intent aesthetic procedure queries in your catchment area. |
| **Google Business Profile / Maps** | Complete listing creation, verification support, service categories, hours, and photo upload for local search dominance. |
| **Facebook Business + Instagram Business** | Business manager setup, page creation, pixel installation, and initial branding via Composio integration. |
| **WhatsApp Business API + Booking Bot** | Verified WhatsApp Business API connection. Automated patient triage, FAQ responses, and booking handoff to your calendar. |
| **ElevenLabs Voice Agent (Charlie)** | AI voice agent for inbound call handling, appointment scheduling, and after-hours patient query capture. |
| **Supabase Backend** | HIPAA-aligned CRM, appointment database, patient analytics, and audit logging. |
| **4 Doctor Profile Pages** | Individual practitioner pages with credentials, specialties, patient reviews, and booking CTAs. |
| **GoodLink Integration** | Telemedicine and EHR bridge for secure video consultations and clinical record continuity. |

**Phase A Delivery Estimate:** 2–3 weeks from signed agreement and deposit.

### Phase B — Ongoing SaaS Management (Monthly Retainer)

After go-live, Studex Group operates as your fully managed digital department. No hiring. No juggling five vendors. One partner, full accountability.

| Service | Inclusions |
|---------|------------|
| **Website Hosting + Maintenance** | Vercel edge hosting, SSL, uptime monitoring, security patches, content updates. |
| **Social Media Management** | Content creation, scheduling, and multi-platform posting via Blotato (Instagram, Facebook, and connected channels). |
| **Google Ads Management** | Campaign optimization, negative keyword sculpting, bid management, and monthly performance tuning. |
| **Meta Ads Management** | Facebook and Instagram ad creative, audience testing, and retargeting pipeline management. |
| **AI Agent Monitoring + Training** | Daily oversight of Naledi (social), Charlie (voice), and WhatsApp bot accuracy. Continuous prompt and workflow refinement. |
| **Monthly Analytics Report** | Executive dashboard snapshot: patient leads, ad ROAS, website traffic, social growth, and bot conversation metrics. |
| **WhatsApp Bot Management** | Response tuning, escalation rules, and patient triage logic updates based on clinic feedback. |
| **Content Automation (RALF Loop)** | Daily autonomous content generation, approval queue, and scheduled publishing across all connected platforms. |
